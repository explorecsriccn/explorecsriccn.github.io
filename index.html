Welcome

The Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IIT Roorkee), India, is thrilled to partner with Google Research to offer the ExploreCSR program, providing a valuable research experience for undergraduate students. We wholeheartedly encourage applications from students belonging to historically underrepresented groups, including women, first-generation college students, and those from low socioeconomic backgrounds. Our main goal is to introduce students to the diverse aspects of computing research by engaging with faculty from various top-ranking institutes and universities. We aspire to instill confidence in them to pursue research careers, equip them with practical skills, and nurture a sense of belonging within the computing research community.

This workshop covers the following research areas:

  • Intelligent Transportation System
  • Networking
  • Computational Mathematics
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Computer Vision
  • Natural Language Processing

About

Who can apply
Undergraduate students in their pre-final or final year are eligible to apply for this workshop. It will be held in both virtual and physical formats this year.
Experience needed
No prior research experience is necessary to participate. However, having completed coursework in introductory computer science, calculus, linear algebra, and probability can be beneficial.
Research activities
(1) Workshop participants will be paired with one or two faculty mentors, along with their Teaching Assistant, who will guide them through the entire workshop and beyond.
(2) Various research projects are available, ranging from literature reviews to replicating prior work, enhancing existing research projects, or embarking on new independent research endeavors. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their work through poster presentations at the end of June 2024 during the physical event. There is also the potential for extending and publishing these projects as short papers.
Time commitment
The workshop spans seven days, with three days conducted virtually, followed by four days taking place on the IIT Roorkee campus. During this event, 10-12 faculty members, each accompanied by their Teaching Assistant, will be actively involved in presenting and interacting with the students. We anticipate that research group meetings, mentor sessions, and project work will require a minimum commitment of 4 hours per week from the beginning to the end of the workshop.
Stipend
To compensate for the opportunity cost incurred while attending the physical workshop, each participant will receive a stipend of up to 10,000 INR, as well as some additional goodies.
